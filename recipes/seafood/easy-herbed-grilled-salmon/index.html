Easy Herbed Grilled Salmon

Foil-grilled salmon with white wine, herbs, garlic, and paprika.

More Seafood seafood salmon grilled foil

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 lb salmon fillet
  • 1 tbsp butter or margarine
  • 2 tbsp white wine
  • 1/2 tsp salt, optional
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp lemon pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp parsley flakes
  • 1/4 tsp sweet Hungarian paprika

Instructions

  1. Make a tray out of a doubled length of heavy-duty foil large enough for the fish fillet.
  2. Fold all four sides up to hold the fish and juices.
  3. Spray the foil tray with cooking spray.
  4. Place a thin line of butter down the center and set the salmon on top.
  5. Squeeze lemon juice over the fish, then add a splash of white wine.
  6. Sprinkle with the seasonings and herbs.
  7. Transfer the foil tray to a hot covered grill.
  8. Cook for 10 minutes per inch of thickness, until the salmon turns light pink and feels firm when pressed gently with the back of a fork.
  9. Serve hot.

Notes

  • The source article says the foil method helps keep the grill clean.
