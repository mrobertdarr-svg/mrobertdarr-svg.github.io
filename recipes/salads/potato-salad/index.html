Potato Salad

A chilled potato salad with eggs, onion, parsley, dill, and a tangy mayo-mustard dressing.

More Salads salad side potato chilled vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 5 large potatoes
  • 1/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • dill, to taste
  • parsley, to taste
  • 1-3 hardboiled eggs, cut up
  • 1/2 sweet onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp mustard (country Dijon recommended)
  • 1/2-1 cup mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Boil whole, washed potatoes with the skins on until tender.
  2. Remove the skins and slice the potatoes into a large bowl.
  3. Add the hardboiled eggs, onion, parsley, and dill, then toss lightly.
  4. Add the oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, mustard, and mayonnaise.
  5. Mix together and chill before serving.
  6. Serve cold.

Notes

  • The dressing should be enough to coat all of the potatoes without making them swim.
  • Optional method: beat together the vinegar, oil, sugar, salt, and pepper first and add it to the potatoes before the mayo and mustard. Let the potatoes marinate for 1 hour, then add the remaining ingredients.
