Spinach and Strawberry Salad

Fresh spinach and strawberries tossed with a sweet tangy dressing and seeds.

More Salads salad spinach strawberry chilled vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches spinach, rinsed and torn up
  • 4 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1/2 cup walnuts (optional)
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup white wine vinegar or balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp poppy seeds

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, toss together the spinach and strawberries.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the oil, vinegar, sugar, paprika, sesame seeds, and poppy seeds.
  3. Pour the dressing over the spinach and strawberries and toss to coat.
  4. Serve chilled.

Notes

  • Variation: leave out the seeds if desired and replace the walnuts or almonds.
  • Variation: add mandarin oranges and avocado slices.
  • Variation: add shredded mozzarella cheese or crumbled feta or blue cheese.
