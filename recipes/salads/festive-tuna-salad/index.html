Festive Tuna Salad

A composed tuna salad with chopped vegetables, lemon, olive oil, and seared tuna steaks.

More Salads salad tuna seafood seared

Ingredients

Tuna Steaks

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • salt, to taste
  • black pepper, to taste
  • 4 tuna steaks, 6-8 oz each and about 1 inch thick

Salsa

  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup diced seeded tomato
  • 1/2 cup diced pineapple
  • 3 scallions, very thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp finely diced jalapeno
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the tuna steaks and turn to coat.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the cucumber, tomato, red onion, olives, parsley, and capers.
  4. Set the salad mixture aside for about 1 hour.
  5. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat for 4-5 minutes.
  6. Sear the tuna for about 3 minutes on the first side and 1 minute on the second side for medium-rare.
  7. Transfer the tuna to individual plates.
  8. Arrange the salad around the fish and serve.
