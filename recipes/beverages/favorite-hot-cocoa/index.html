Favorite Hot Cocoa

Classic stovetop hot cocoa with a microwave single-serving variation.

More Beverages beverage cocoa stovetop microwave vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup Hershey's cocoa
  • dash of salt
  • 1/3 cup hot water
  • 4 cups milk
  • 3/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix sugar, cocoa, and salt in a saucepan.
  2. Stir in the hot water.
  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring until the mixture boils.
  4. Boil and stir for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in the milk and heat gently without boiling.
  6. Remove from heat and add the vanilla extract.
  7. Serve hot.

Notes

  • Makes 6 servings.
  • Microwave single serving: combine 2 tbsp sugar, 2-3 tsp cocoa, and a dash of salt in a large mug. Heat 1 cup milk in the microwave on high for 1 1/2 minutes or until hot, then gradually stir it into the cocoa mixture and add 1/4 tsp vanilla extract.
