Ice-Cream Punch

Creamy sherbet-style party punch made with vanilla ice cream, lemonade, and Sprite.

More Beverages beverage punch party cold

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts vanilla ice cream or favorite sherbet flavor
  • 1 can water
  • 1 12-oz can frozen lemonade concentrate, thawed
  • 1 2-liter bottle Sprite

Instructions

  1. Spoon the ice cream into a large punch bowl by tablespoonfuls.
  2. Add the water and lemonade concentrate.
  3. Stir until just combined.
  4. Slowly pour the Sprite down the side of the bowl and stir gently to mix.
  5. Serve cold.

Notes

  • Makes about 32 4-oz servings.
