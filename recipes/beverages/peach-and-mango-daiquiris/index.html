Peach and Mango Daiquiris

Frozen peach-mango daiquiris blended until smooth and served ice cold.

More Beverages beverage cocktail frozen peach mango vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup frozen mango-peach juice concentrate, thawed
  • 3/4 cup white rum
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1-lb bag frozen unsweetened sliced peaches
  • fresh mint sprigs

Instructions

  1. Combine the mango-peach juice concentrate, rum, orange juice, and sugar in a blender.
  2. Add half of the frozen peaches and puree.
  3. Add the remaining peaches a few at a time, blending until the mixture is smooth and thick and stopping to stir as needed.
  4. Pour into chilled daiquiri glasses or wine glasses.
  5. Garnish with mint leaves and serve cold.
