Lemon Dijon Wings

Chicken wings marinated in olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and garlic, then grilled.

More Marinades marinade chicken wings grilled

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp style Dijon mustard
  • 6 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1-2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 lb chicken wings, separated

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, stir together the olive oil, lemon juice, mustard, and garlic.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the chicken wings and marinate for at least 2 hours, turning occasionally.
  4. Preheat and lightly oil the grill grate.
  5. Grill the wings for 15-20 minutes, turning frequently and basting with marinade every few minutes.
  6. Serve hot.

Notes

  • The source appears to intend a style of Dijon mustard; the exact wording before Dijon is partially unclear.
