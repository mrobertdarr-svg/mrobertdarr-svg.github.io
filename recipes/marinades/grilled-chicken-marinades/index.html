Grilled Chicken Marinades

Two marinade blends for grilled chicken: blackened chicken and honey BBQ chicken.

More Marinades marinade chicken grilling seasoning

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/8 tsp white pepper
  • 1/8 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2/3 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • dash cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. For blackened chicken, mix the paprika, salt, cayenne pepper, cumin, thyme, white pepper, and onion powder.
  2. Rub the blackened seasoning onto chicken wings, breasts, or pieces before grilling.
  3. For honey BBQ chicken, combine the brown sugar, kosher salt, garlic powder, cinnamon, butter, ketchup, honey, balsamic vinegar, soy sauce, and cayenne pepper.
  4. Coat the chicken with the honey BBQ marinade before grilling.

Notes

  • The source page labels these as marinades for chicken wings, breasts, or other grilled chicken pieces.
