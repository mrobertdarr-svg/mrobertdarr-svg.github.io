Thousand Island Dressing

Quick homemade Thousand Island dressing that tastes better than store-bought.

More Dressings dressing sauce salad chilled vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp sweet pickle relish
  • 1 tsp sweet onion, finely minced
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 dash black pepper

Instructions

  1. Stir all of the ingredients together in a small bowl.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Place the dressing in a covered container and refrigerate for several hours, stirring occasionally so the sugar dissolves and the flavors blend.

Notes

  • Ready in about 10 minutes.
  • Serves 5.
