Best Homemade Pizza Sauce

Quick homemade pizza sauce made from canned tomatoes, garlic, basil, and oregano.

More Sauces sauce pizza tomato stovetop

Ingredients

  • 1 28-ounce can whole tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated onion or finely minced onion
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1/4-1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 3 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Pour the tomatoes into a large bowl and break them down by hand or with kitchen shears, leaving them slightly chunky.
  2. Place a medium saucepan over medium heat and add the olive oil.
  3. Add the onions, oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring often.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  5. Add the prepared tomatoes, sugar, and salt.
  6. Let the sauce come to a simmer, then lower the heat to medium-low and cook for 30 minutes.
  7. Taste and adjust the seasoning as desired.

Notes

  • Makes about 2 1/2 cups.
