No-Rise Pizza Dough

Fast all-purpose pizza dough that skips the waiting time and bakes right away.

More Pizza pizza dough bread baked vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 0.25-ounce package active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water, 110°F or 45°C
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine the flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Mix in the warm water and oil until the dough comes together.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  5. Pat and stretch the dough onto a large pizza pan.
  6. Cover with desired toppings.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the crust is lightly browned.
