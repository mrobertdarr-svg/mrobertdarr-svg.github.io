How to Make Pizza

Simple homemade pizza assembly notes using dough, sauce, cheese, and optional pepperoni.

More Pizza pizza how-to baked cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 pack pizza dough
  • 1 pizza sauce
  • 1 container cheese
  • 1 container tomato sauce
  • 1 knife
  • 1 rolling pin
  • 10 pepperonis, optional

Instructions

  1. Get out the pizza dough, rolling pin, and flour.
  2. Sprinkle some flour on the rolling pin.
  3. Spread the pizza dough into a large circle.
  4. Put the dough in the oven for 1-5 minutes.
  5. Add the tomato sauce.
  6. Add the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Add 10 pepperonis if you want them.
  8. Return the pizza to the oven for 5-10 minutes.
  9. Remove when done and serve.
