Ham and Bean Soup

Simple slow-simmered bean soup with ham hock, vegetables, and bay leaf.

More Soups soup beans ham simmered

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb dried small white navy beans
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 ham hock
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 1 bay leaf

Instructions

  1. Add the beans, water, ham hock, onion, carrot, pepper, celery, bouillon, and bay leaf to a large pot.
  2. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Reduce the heat and slow simmer for about 3 hours.
  4. Serve hot.
