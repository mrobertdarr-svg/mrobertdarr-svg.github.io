Summer Pea Soup

Light pea soup with onion, chicken broth, and a sour cream or yogurt finish.

More Soups soup peas chilled stovetop

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp chopped onion
  • 2 10-oz packages frozen green peas, thawed
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 4 tbsp sour cream or plain yogurt
  • fresh mint sprigs or sliced scallions, optional

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan.
  2. Saute the onion until tender, about 8 minutes.
  3. Add the peas, broth, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a food processor and process until smooth.
  6. Refrigerate to chill slightly.
  7. Top each serving with 2 tsp sour cream or yogurt and garnish with mint sprigs or scallions.
  8. Serve slightly chilled.
