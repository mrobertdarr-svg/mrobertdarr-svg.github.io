🥗 Cucumber & Tomato Salad (Fresh, Tangy)

Family recipe.

More Side Dishes

Ingredients

Instructions

1. Prep

  • Slice cucumbers
  • Halve tomatoes
  • Slice onion
  • Add everything to a bowl

2. Make dressing

Whisk together:

  • olive oil
  • vinegar
  • salt
  • pepper
  • sugar

3. Toss

  • Pour dressing over vegetables
  • Mix well

4. Let it sit (important)

  • Let sit 10–15 minutes before serving
