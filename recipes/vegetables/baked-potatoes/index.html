Baked Potatoes

Simple oven-baked potatoes with crispy skin.

More Vegetables side oven

Ingredients

  • 4 russet potatoes
  • olive oil
  • kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Scrub and dry potatoes.
  3. Rub with oil and salt.
  4. Bake 75 minutes.

Notes

  • Place directly on rack for crispier skin.
