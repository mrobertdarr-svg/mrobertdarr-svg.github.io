Aunt Laurie's Cheesy Potatoes

Baked cheesy hash brown casserole with a buttery corn flake topping.

More Vegetables vegetable potato casserole baked

Ingredients

  • 32-oz package of hash browns or potato puffs
  • 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • small chopped onion, optional
  • 2 cups corn flakes, optional

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Put the potatoes in a large 9 x 13 casserole dish.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, stir together the soup, milk, sour cream, cheese, and 3/4 stick melted butter.
  4. Add the salt, pepper, and onion.
  5. Pour the mixture into the casserole dish with the potatoes and stir until the potatoes are evenly covered.
  6. Put the corn flakes in a smaller bowl and crush them with a fork.
  7. Mix the crushed corn flakes with 1/4 stick melted butter.
  8. Spread the corn flake mixture on top of the potatoes.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes and serve hot.
