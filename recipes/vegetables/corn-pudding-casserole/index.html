Corn Pudding Casserole

Creamy baked corn casserole made with whole-kernel corn, cream-style corn, muffin mix, and sour cream.

More Vegetables vegetable corn casserole baked

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15.25 oz) whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 can (8 oz) cream-style corn
  • 1 box (8.5 oz) corn muffin mix
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • dash of salt, to taste
  • dash of pepper, to taste
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk the eggs.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients and mix well.
  4. Spoon the mixture into a 7 x 11-inch or 10 x 10-inch baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray.
  5. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
  6. Cut into bars and serve hot with butter.
