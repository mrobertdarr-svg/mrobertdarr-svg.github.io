Microwave Baked Potatoes

Quick microwave baked potatoes with optional sweet potato variation and classic toppings.

More Vegetables vegetable potato microwave side

Ingredients

  • baking potatoes or sweet potatoes
  • butter
  • sour cream
  • chives
  • bacon bits
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • butter, cinnamon, and sugar for sweet potatoes

Instructions

  1. Scrub the potatoes clean and pat them dry.
  2. Using a fork, prick holes in each potato.
  3. Arrange the potatoes on a microwave-safe plate.
  4. Microwave uncovered on high until tender:
  5. Cook 5-7 minutes for 1 potato, 7-9 minutes for 2 potatoes, or 13-16 minutes for 4 potatoes.
  6. Turn the potatoes over once about halfway through cooking.
  7. Let the potatoes cool for about 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with the desired toppings and serve hot.

Notes
