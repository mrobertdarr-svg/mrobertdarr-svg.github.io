Mashed Potatoes

Classic mashed potatoes beaten with butter, salt, pepper, and milk until creamy.

More Vegetables vegetable potato side stovetop vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 6 medium russet or brown potatoes
  • 2-3 tbsp butter
  • dash of salt
  • dash of pepper
  • milk, about 1/2 cup or more if needed

Instructions

  1. Peel and quarter the potatoes.
  2. Cook the potatoes in boiling water for 20-25 minutes or until tender.
  3. Drain the potatoes.
  4. Put the potatoes in a large mixing bowl and beat with an electric mixer on low speed.
  5. Add butter, salt, and pepper to taste.
  6. Gradually beat in the milk until the potatoes become creamy, light, and fluffy.
  7. Serve hot with melted butter or gravy.

Notes

  • The source image includes a handwritten note increasing the milk from about 1 tbsp to about 1/2 cup or more if the potatoes seem dry.
