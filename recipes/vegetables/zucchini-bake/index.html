Zucchini Bake

Baked zucchini casserole with crescent roll dough, herbs, eggs, and cheese.

More Vegetables vegetable zucchini casserole baked vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 packages refrigerated crescent roll dough
  • 8 cups thinly sliced zucchini
  • 2 cups chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Spread the dough into and up the sides of a 9 x 13 baking dish sprayed with nonstick spray.
  3. Sprinkle all the sesame seeds on top and set aside.
  4. Melt the butter in a skillet.
  5. Saute the zucchini, onion, parsley, garlic, pepper, basil, and oregano until the zucchini is tender.
  6. Combine the eggs, both cheeses, and salt in a bowl.
  7. Gently toss the zucchini mixture with the egg mixture.
  8. Pour the filling into the crust.
  9. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the center is set.
  10. Serve hot.
