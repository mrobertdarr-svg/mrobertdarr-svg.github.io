Avocado-Cilantro Deviled Eggs

Creamy deviled eggs blended with avocado, cilantro, lime, and sour cream.

More Appetizers appetizer eggs avocado chilled

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs, hard-boiled and halved
  • 1 1/2 avocados, peeled and pit removed
  • 3/4 cup low-fat sour cream
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, plus extra for garnish
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp celery salt
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • zest of lime
  • paprika, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cut the hard-boiled eggs in half, separating the yolks from the whites.
  2. Set the egg whites aside and put the yolks in a blender.
  3. Add the avocados and blend.
  4. Add the sour cream, cilantro, black pepper, salt, celery salt, lime juice, and lime zest.
  5. Blend until smooth.
  6. Fill a pastry bag with a decorating tip or use a plastic bag with the tip cut off and pipe the mixture into the egg whites.
  7. Garnish with extra cilantro and a sprinkle of paprika.

Notes

  • Makes 12 servings.
