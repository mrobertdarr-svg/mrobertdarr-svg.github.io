Guacamole Dip

Simple guacamole-style dip mixed with salsa, sour cream, and lemon juice.

More Appetizers appetizer dip avocado no-cook vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • salsa, to taste
  • sour cream
  • lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Pit the avocados and remove the peels.
  2. In a serving bowl, mash the avocados with a fork.
  3. Slowly mix in salsa to taste, about 1/2 of a regular-sized jar.
  4. Add some sour cream, about 2 tbsp, to make it lighter.
  5. Add a bit of lemon juice, about 1 tsp, to keep the dip from turning brown.
  6. Mix until the dip is a dull green color.
  7. Serve at room temperature with tortilla chips.

Notes

  • The source mentions Old El Paso salsa.
