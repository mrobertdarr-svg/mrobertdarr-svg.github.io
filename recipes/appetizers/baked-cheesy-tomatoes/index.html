Baked Cheesy Tomatoes

Baked tomato halves topped with Parmesan, mozzarella, basil, and oregano.

More Appetizers appetizer tomatoes cheese baked vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup freshly shredded mozzarella
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh oregano
  • salt, to taste
  • freshly ground pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F.
  2. Place the tomato halves cut-side up on a baking sheet.
  3. Top with Parmesan and mozzarella.
  4. Sprinkle on the basil and oregano.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake until the tomatoes are tender, about 15 minutes.
