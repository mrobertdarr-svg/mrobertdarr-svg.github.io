Mac and Cheese II

Baked macaroni and cheese with cheddar and processed cheese food.

More Main Dishes main-dish pasta macaroni baked

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 oz cubed processed cheese food

Instructions

  1. Cook the elbow macaroni in boiling salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter or margarine.
  3. Add the flour and milk and cook until thick and bubbly, about 6 to 7 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Add the cheeses and stir until the cheese sauce is smooth.
  5. Toss the cooked macaroni with the cheese sauce to coat evenly.
  6. Pour into a greased 2-quart casserole dish.
  7. Bake in a preheated 350°F oven for 30 minutes.
  8. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Notes

  • Makes 4 servings.
