Pizza Casserole

Handwritten pizza casserole layered with shells, hamburger, sauce, and mozzarella.

More Main Dishes main-dish casserole pizza baked

Ingredients

  • 1 10 oz box large macaroni shells
  • 4 lb browned hamburger
  • 4 oz pepperoni
  • 80 oz shells
  • 14 oz jar pizza sauce
  • 15 oz can tomato sauce
  • 15 oz white sauce
  • 12 oz shredded mozzarella, your preference

Instructions

  1. Cook the shells until tender.
  2. Brown the hamburger.
  3. Add the pizza sauce, tomato sauce, and seasonings.
  4. Layer the shells, meat, and sauce in a casserole dish.
  5. Add pepperoni and cheese.
  6. Bake at 300°F until hot and bubbly, or finish in a crockpot as noted in the source.
