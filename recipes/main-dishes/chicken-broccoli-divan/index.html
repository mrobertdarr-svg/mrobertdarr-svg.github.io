Chicken Broccoli Divan

Quick baked chicken and broccoli casserole with cream of chicken soup and cheddar.

More Main Dishes main-dish chicken broccoli casserole

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked broccoli flowerets
  • 1 1/2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 1 can 10 3/4 oz Campbell's Cream of Chicken Soup or 98% Fat Free Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp dry bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp butter or margarine, melted

Instructions

  1. Place the broccoli and chicken in a 9-inch pie plate or 2-quart shallow baking dish.
  2. Mix the soup and milk and pour over.
  3. Sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Mix the bread crumbs with butter and sprinkle on top.
  5. Bake at 450°F for 20 minutes or until hot.
  6. Serve hot.

Notes
