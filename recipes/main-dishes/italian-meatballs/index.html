Italian Meatballs

Baked beef meatballs with breadcrumbs, herbs, and a mozzarella-Parmesan finish.

More Main Dishes main-dish meatballs beef baked

Ingredients

  • Italian bread crumbs
  • Italian bread crumbs
  • powdered Parmesan cheese
  • fresh Parmesan cheese
  • ground beef
  • oregano
  • marjoram
  • basil
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • parsley
  • salt
  • Parmesan cheese
  • mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Combine the bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, ground beef, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, parsley, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add a little milk and shape the mixture into meatballs.
  4. Add an extra pinch of oregano and basil if desired, then add a little onion powder, garlic powder, and a pinch of nutmeg.
  5. Coat the meatballs with more bread crumb mixture.
  6. Brown the meatballs with 2-4 tbsp butter in a stovetop pan.
  7. When browned, place them in a baking dish and cover with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake uncovered for 45 minutes, then bake 5 minutes more.
  9. Serve with spaghetti or on hoagie rolls as meatball subs.
