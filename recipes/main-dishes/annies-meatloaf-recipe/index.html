Annie's Meatloaf Recipe

Classic beef meatloaf baked with tomato sauce.

More Main Dishes main-dish meatloaf beef baked

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground round steak or ground beef
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups plain bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 3/4 cup Lipton onion soup mix
  • 3/4 cup diced celery
  • 3/4 cup diced onions

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly in a large bowl.
  2. Place the mixture in a pan.
  3. Cover with tomato sauce.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
