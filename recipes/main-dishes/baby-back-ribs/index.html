Baby Back Ribs

Oven-grilled baby back ribs coated with a brown sugar mustard spice rub and barbecue sauce.

More Main Dishes main-dish ribs pork grilled

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp prepared mustard
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 racks pork baby back ribs (3 1/2 to 4 lb)
  • 1/3 to 1/2 cup barbecue sauce

Instructions

  1. Combine the brown sugar, mustard, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Rub the mixture evenly onto the ribs to coat.
  3. Place the ribs on a cooking grill.
  4. Grill for 35 to 45 minutes or until tender and cooked through.
  5. Turn once and brush with barbecue sauce during the last 10 minutes of grilling.
  6. Serve hot.
