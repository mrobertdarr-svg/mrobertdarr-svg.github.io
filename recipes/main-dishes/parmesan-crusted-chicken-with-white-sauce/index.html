Parmesan Crusted Chicken with White Sauce

Breaded chicken served over bowtie pasta with a creamy white wine Asiago sauce.

More Main Dishes main-dish chicken pasta creamy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain or Italian bread crumbs
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup milk
  • 6 chicken breast strips or chicken tenders
  • vegetable oil
  • 2 cups dry bowtie pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves sliced garlic
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 3/4 cup half-and-half
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp basil
  • 3/4 cup mild Asiago cheese, finely grated
  • 4 broccoli florets, lightly steamed
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pasta according to the package directions.
  2. Wash and drain the chicken strips.
  3. Mix the bread crumbs, flour, and Parmesan cheese together.
  4. Place the milk in a shallow dish for dipping.
  5. Dip the chicken in the milk, then in the crumb mixture.
  6. Fry the chicken in heated oil over medium-low heat until golden, then remove and drain.
  7. In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter, then add the olive oil.
  8. Quickly add the wine and garlic and stir well.
  9. Add the half-and-half and sour cream and stir until smooth.
  10. Add the Asiago cheese and stir until melted.
  11. Sprinkle in the basil and stir lightly.
  12. Divide the pasta into serving dishes and spoon the sauce over the pasta.
  13. Top with mushrooms or broccoli if desired, place the chicken on top, and finish with crushed red pepper and extra Parmesan cheese if desired.
  14. Serve hot.
