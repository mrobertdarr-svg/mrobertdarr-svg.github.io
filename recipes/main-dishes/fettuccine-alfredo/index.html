Fettuccine Alfredo

Rich Alfredo sauce tossed with hot fettuccine noodles.

More Main Dishes main-dish pasta alfredo creamy

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2/3 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lb prepared fettuccine noodles

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter.
  2. Add the cream cheese, heavy cream, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly.
  4. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Serve over hot fettuccine noodles.
