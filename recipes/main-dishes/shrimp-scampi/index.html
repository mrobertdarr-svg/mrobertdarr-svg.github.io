Shrimp Scampi

Handwritten shrimp scampi-style dish served with angel hair or linguine.

More Main Dishes main-dish shrimp seafood pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs shrimp, peeled and cleaned
  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • king of parsley
  • 2 tsp dry white wine
  • full teaspoon pepper and/or parsley
  • basil, oregano, and garlic powder
  • serve on angel hair or linguine

Instructions

  1. Peel and clean the shrimp.
  2. Mix with olive oil, chopped garlic, and seasonings.
  3. Add the wine and let the mixture marinate.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 10 to 20 minutes.
  5. Serve on angel hair or linguine.

Notes

  • The handwritten source is hard to decipher in several places, so this recipe preserves the clearly legible ingredient phrases and main method.
