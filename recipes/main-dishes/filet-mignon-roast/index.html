Filet Mignon Roast

Beef tenderloin roast marinated in a tomato-based barbecue sauce.

More Main Dishes main-dish beef roast oven

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds beef tenderloin
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice or cider vinegar
  • 4 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp dry mustard
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. For the barbecue sauce, mix the tomato puree through the garlic powder in a small saucepan.
  2. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Marinate the beef tenderloin in the barbecue sauce for 4 hours in the refrigerator.
  4. Place the tenderloin on a rack in a roasting pan and pour over the marinade.
  5. Place in a cold oven.
  6. Heat the oven to 400°F and roast the meat for 1 hour, or until the meat reaches the desired doneness.
  7. Remove to a warm platter, slice, and serve.

Notes

  • Serves 12.
