Grilled Tuna Steaks with Strawberry Salsa

Grilled tuna steaks served with a bright strawberry-jalapeno salsa.

More Main Dishes main-dish tuna grilled salsa

Ingredients

  • 1 cup lightly packed fresh basil leaves
  • 2/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • 4 tuna steaks, 6 oz each
  • 1/2 medium red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, minced
  • 1/2 cup lightly packed fresh basil leaves, seeded and julienned
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, stemmed, seeded, and julienned
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh mint
  • 1/2 cup finely shredded fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1/2 pint fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • dash of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Place the basil leaves, olive oil, vinegar, soy sauce, pepper, sugar, salt, and hot water in a blender and puree.
  2. Arrange the tuna steaks in a single layer and pour the marinade over them.
  3. Cover and marinate for 3-4 hours in the refrigerator.
  4. Combine the salsa ingredients in a large mixing bowl and toss to combine.
  5. Cover and refrigerate the salsa for at least 2 hours before serving.
  6. Preheat the broiler or a grill for cooking the tuna indoors.
  7. Drain the tuna steaks and grill or broil them, turning once, until charred outside but still pink in the center, about 3 minutes.
  8. Spoon the strawberry salsa over the tuna steaks, garnish with basil sprigs if desired, and serve hot.
