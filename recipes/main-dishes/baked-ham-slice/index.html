Baked Ham Slice

Ham baked with a sweet mustard, nutmeg, cider, and maple syrup glaze.

More Main Dishes main-dish ham baked glaze

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp dry mustard
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 pound 1-inch thick slice of ham
  • 1/2 cup apple cider
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Combine the mustard and nutmeg.
  3. Rub the mixture into both sides of the ham slice.
  4. Place the ham in a baking dish.
  5. Pour the cider and maple syrup over the ham.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes, basting occasionally.
  7. Slice and serve hot.
