Sour Cream Chicken Enchiladas

Chicken enchiladas baked with Monterey Jack cheese and a sour cream green chile sauce.

More Main Dishes main-dish chicken enchiladas baked

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil or canola oil
  • 1 cup finely chopped onion
  • 3 cups cooked shredded chicken
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 8 tortillas
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped green chiles
  • 2 tbsp chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Spray a 9 x 13 baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Saute the onion in oil until softened.
  4. Add the chicken, Monterey Jack cheese, and salt, then toss to combine and let cool.
  5. In a saucepan, melt the butter and add the flour, whisking for about 1 minute.
  6. Gradually whisk in the broth until smooth and bubbling.
  7. Reduce the heat and stir in the sour cream and green chiles without letting the sauce boil.
  8. Place an equal amount of the chicken mixture on each tortilla, roll them up, and place seam-side down in the baking dish.
  9. Pour the sauce evenly over the tortillas and sprinkle with chopped cilantro.
  10. Bake for 30 minutes and serve hot.
