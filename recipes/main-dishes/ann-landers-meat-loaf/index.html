Ann Landers' Meat Loaf

Old-fashioned meat loaf with bacon and tomato sauce.

More Main Dishes main-dish meatloaf beef baked

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 1/2 cups breadcrumbs
  • 3/4 cup beef bouillon
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 package onion soup mix
  • 2 oz bacon, optional

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients except the bacon and tomato sauce in a 9 x 5 x 3-inch loaf pan.
  2. Cover with 2 strips of bacon if using.
  3. Pour tomato sauce over the meat loaf.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 1 1/2 hours.

Notes
