Coconut Cake

Tender coconut cake finished with jam and extra shredded coconut.

More Desserts dessert cake coconut baked

Ingredients

  • butter and flour for the pan
  • 1 stick plus 2 tsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 scant tbsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 1 pinch fleur de sel
  • scant 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 3/4 cups unsweetened shredded coconut, divided
  • generous 1/2 cup blueberry jam or another jam of your choice

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Grease a standard loaf pan with butter and dust it with flour.
  3. Gently melt the butter and pour it into a mixing bowl.
  4. Stir in the brown sugar.
  5. Whisk in the eggs one at a time.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and fleur de sel.
  7. Using a spatula, mix the dry ingredients into the egg mixture.
  8. Gradually add the milk to make a smooth batter.
  9. Fold in a generous cup of the shredded coconut.
  10. Pour the batter into the loaf pan.
  11. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean. Bake 10 minutes longer if needed.
  12. Turn the cake out onto a wire rack and let it cool.
  13. Spread the jam over the top of the cake and sprinkle over the remaining coconut.
  14. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Notes

  • Serves 8-10.
