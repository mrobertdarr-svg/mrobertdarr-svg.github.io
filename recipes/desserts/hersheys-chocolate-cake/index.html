Hershey's Chocolate Cake

Classic Hershey's chocolate cake with a simple chocolate frosting.

More Desserts dessert cake chocolate baked vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup Hershey's cocoa
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  3. Combine the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, then beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin.
  6. Pour into the prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a wooden pick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from the pans and cool completely.
  9. Frost with chocolate frosting.

Notes

  • Cupcake variation: line muffin cups with paper bake cups and fill 2/3 full. Bake at 350°F for 22-25 minutes.
  • Frosting: melt 1 stick butter or margarine and add 2/3 cup Hershey's cocoa, 3 cups powdered sugar, 1/3 cup milk, and 1 tsp vanilla extract. Add more milk if needed to reach spreading consistency.
  • Yields about 2 cups frosting.
