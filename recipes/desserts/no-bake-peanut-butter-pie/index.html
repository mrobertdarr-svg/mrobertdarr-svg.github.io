No-Bake Peanut Butter Pie

Frozen peanut butter pie folded with whipped topping in a graham cracker or Oreo crust.

More Desserts dessert peanut-butter pie no-bake

Ingredients

  • 1 8-oz package cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups confectioner's sugar
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 package frozen Cool Whip, thawed, or 2 cups heavy whipped cream
  • 2 9-inch prepared graham cracker or Oreo pie crusts

Instructions

  1. Beat together the cream cheese and confectioner's sugar.
  2. Mix in the peanut butter, milk, and vanilla and beat until smooth.
  3. Fold in the whipped topping.
  4. Spoon the filling into the two pie crusts.
  5. Cover and freeze until firm.
  6. Serve frozen.

Notes

  • Variation: make a chocolate peanut butter pie by spreading a layer of chocolate fudge on the crust first, or by swirling chocolate syrup into the filling before freezing.
  • Garnish with broken mini Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, chocolate sauce, and whipped cream.
