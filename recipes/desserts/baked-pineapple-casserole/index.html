Baked Pineapple Casserole

Warm pineapple and bread casserole spiced with cinnamon and nutmeg.

More Desserts dessert pineapple casserole baked vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2-3/4 cup sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp cinnamon, or to taste
  • 1 tsp nutmeg, or to taste
  • 1 large can crushed pineapple with juice
  • 6-8 slices bread, cubed

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Lightly grease a medium-sized baking dish.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time.
  5. Stir in the cinnamon and nutmeg.
  6. Stir in the bread and crushed pineapple with juice.
  7. Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish.
  8. Bake for 60 minutes or until bubbly and lightly browned.
  9. Serve hot.
