Apple Burritos

Sweet baked tortillas filled with cinnamon apple filling and a buttery sauce.

More Desserts dessert apples baked tortilla vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 4-6 flour tortillas
  • 5-6 large apples, cored but unpeeled, sliced thin
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 1/4 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • grated fresh lemon peel, optional
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch blended into 2 tbsp lemon or lime juice
  • melted butter, about 1 tbsp per burrito
  • cinnamon, to taste
  • sugar, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F and lightly coat a 9 x 13 baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the sugar and 2 tbsp butter in the water until the mixture begins to simmer.
  3. Add the apple slices and stir in the cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, lemon peel, and vanilla.
  4. Cook until the apples begin to soften, then stir in the cornstarch-lemon mixture until the apples become a bit softer and the sauce thickens.
  5. Put some of the apple filling down the center of each tortilla and wrap the tortillas like a burrito or enchilada.
  6. Put the burritos into the baking dish.
  7. Pour melted butter over the burritos, coating the tops.
  8. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
  9. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the tops are golden brown and the bottoms are bubbly.
  10. Serve hot.

Notes

  • Recommended garnishes: melted cheddar cheese with sour cream for breakfast, or vanilla ice cream for dessert.
