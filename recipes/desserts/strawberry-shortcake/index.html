Strawberry Shortcake

Simple shortcake topped with whipped cream and sugared strawberries.

More Desserts dessert strawberry shortcake baked

Ingredients

  • 1 lb strawberries, halved and sugared
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream, whipped with sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
  2. Brush an 8-inch round cake pan with a small amount of butter and coat the base and sides with a little flour.
  3. Combine the flour, salt, sugar, and baking powder in a large bowl.
  4. Cut in the butter until coarse crumbs form.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the eggs and milk.
  6. Add the egg mixture to the dry ingredients and stir until just moistened.
  7. Spread the batter in the prepared cake pan.
  8. Bake for 20 minutes.
  9. Turn the cake out onto a wire rack to cool.
  10. Serve in pieces with whipped cream and strawberries on top.

Notes

  • Sugaring the strawberries a day ahead is recommended so the juices draw out and soak into the shortcake when served.
