Banana Bread

Classic banana bread baked in two loaf pans with optional chopped walnuts.

More Desserts dessert bread banana baked

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups sifted flour
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups ripe mashed bananas (5-6 bananas)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Sift together the flour, baking soda, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Mash the bananas in a separate bowl and add the lemon juice.
  4. In a third bowl, cream the shortening and sugar with an electric mixer at medium speed.
  5. Add the eggs and beat until very light and fluffy, about 4 minutes total.
  6. Add the dry ingredients alternately with the milk.
  7. Fold in the bananas and nuts.
  8. Pour into two greased 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 x 2 1/2-inch loaf pans.
  9. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
  10. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on wire racks.
  11. Wrap in foil or plastic to store and serve at room temperature.
