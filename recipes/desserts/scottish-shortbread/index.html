Scottish Shortbread

Simple buttery shortbread pressed into a pan and baked until lightly golden.

More Desserts dessert cookies shortbread baked

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup cornstarch or rice flour
  • 4 sticks butter
  • 8 tbsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Blend the butter with a fork.
  2. Add the sugar and blend.
  3. Add the flour and cornstarch.
  4. Blend into a ball.
  5. Pat the dough into an ungreased cookie sheet or pie pan.
  6. Press the dough down with a fork.
  7. Bake at 350°F for 10-20 minutes until lightly golden.
  8. Cut while warm and let cool.

Notes

  • The handwritten note says not to overmix.
