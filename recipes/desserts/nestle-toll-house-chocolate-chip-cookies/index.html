Nestle Toll House Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic chocolate chip cookies made with butter, brown sugar, and semisweet chocolate chips.

More Desserts dessert cookies chocolate baked vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Beat the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla in a large mixer bowl until creamy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually beat in the flour mixture.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Let stand for 2 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
