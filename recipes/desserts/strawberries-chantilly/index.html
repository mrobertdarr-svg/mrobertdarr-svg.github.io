Strawberries Chantilly

Fresh strawberries served chilled with lightly sweetened vanilla whipped cream.

More Desserts dessert strawberries whipped-cream chilled

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2-1 tsp vanilla
  • 1-3 tbsp sifted confectioner's sugar or granulated sugar, if necessary
  • 1 or more cleaned strawberries

Instructions

  1. Whip the cream with an electric beater until it is slightly stiff.
  2. Fold in half of the vanilla and all of the sugar.
  3. Continue whipping, slowly adding the rest of the vanilla and sugar until the cream is stiff.
  4. Serve chilled with clean, whole strawberries.
