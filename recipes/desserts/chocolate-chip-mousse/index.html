Chocolate Chip Mousse

Light chilled chocolate mousse made with melted semisweet chocolate and whipped cream.

More Desserts dessert mousse chocolate chilled vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 6 oz semisweet chocolate chips
  • 6 tbsp sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs, room temperature if possible

Instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate chips in a double boiler on the stove and stir often.
  2. Beat the eggs on low, then sprinkle in the sugar.
  3. Beat on high until the eggs become thick and light yellow.
  4. Pour the melted chocolate into the eggs and beat on low until blended.
  5. In a separate bowl, beat the cream and vanilla together on high until the mixture is very thick but not overmixed.
  6. Fold 1/4 of the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture, then repeat until all the cream is folded in.
  7. Separate into dessert cups and chill for several hours.
  8. Serve cold.

Notes

  • The source mentions butter in the folding step, but the visible ingredient list does not include it; this entry follows the clearly listed ingredients.
