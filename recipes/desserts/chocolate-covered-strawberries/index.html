Chocolate-Covered Strawberries

Fresh strawberries dipped in melted chocolate and chilled until firm.

More Desserts dessert strawberries chocolate no-bake

Ingredients

  • 16 oz milk chocolate chips
  • 6 tbsp shortening
  • 12 strawberries, dried very well after cleaning
  • toothpicks or party sticks
  • Styrofoam or wax paper for cooling the strawberries

Instructions

  1. Insert toothpicks into the tops of the strawberries.
  2. Melt the chocolate and shortening in a double boiler, stirring occasionally until smooth.
  3. Holding each strawberry by the toothpick, dip it into the chocolate mixture.
  4. Turn the strawberries upside down and insert the toothpick into Styrofoam, allowing the chocolate to cool and harden.
  5. Serve at room temperature or slightly chilled.

Notes

  • The source suggests party sticks as a stronger option than toothpicks.
