Top 10 Panini Combinations

ideas/top-10-panini-combinations.md

1. Prosciutto, Mozzarella & Basil (Caprese + Ham)

  • Prosciutto
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Tomato
  • Basil
  • Olive oil or balsamic

Comment: Clean, salty, and fresh. Best on ciabatta or focaccia.

2. Ham, Brie & Apple

  • Ham
  • Brie
  • Thin apple slices
  • Dijon mustard or honey

Comment: Sweet + creamy + savory. Very popular café-style sandwich.

3. Turkey, Swiss & Dijon

  • Turkey
  • Swiss cheese
  • Dijon mustard
  • Tomato
  • Optional red onion

Comment: Classic deli flavor with a sharp bite from Dijon.

4. Avocado, Mozzarella & Tomato

  • Avocado
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato
  • Arugula
  • Lemon juice or balsamic

Comment: Creamy and fresh. Add red pepper flakes for a kick.

5. Italian Combo (Salami, Ham, Provolone)

  • Salami
  • Ham
  • Provolone
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Italian dressing

Comment: Like a pressed Italian sub — bold and salty.

6. Chicken Pesto & Mozzarella

  • Grilled chicken
  • Mozzarella
  • Pesto
  • Tomato

Comment: Herby, melty, and very popular in fast-casual restaurants.

7. Cubano (Cuban Panini)

  • Roast pork
  • Ham
  • Swiss cheese
  • Pickles
  • Mustard

Comment: Tangy and crispy. Press firmly for best results.

8. Roast Beef & Cheddar with Horseradish

  • Roast beef
  • Sharp cheddar
  • Horseradish sauce
  • Optional caramelized onions

Comment: Rich with a strong kick. Great on sourdough.

9. Four Cheese & Tomato

  • Mozzarella
  • Cheddar
  • Parmesan
  • Provolone
  • Tomato

Comment: Elevated grilled cheese. Super melty and comforting.

10. Spicy Turkey & Pepper Jack

  • Turkey
  • Pepper jack cheese
  • Jalapeños
  • Chipotle mayo

Comment: Smoky, spicy, and bold. Add avocado to mellow heat.

General Panini Tips

  • Put cheese near the bread for better melting
  • Use medium heat to avoid burning before melting
  • Lightly oil or butter the outside for crisp texture
  • Let rest 1 minute before cutting
